# AI-Powered Banking Customer Intelligence & Decision Support System

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior, transaction activity, campaign performance, and churn trends for a retail banking environment.

The goal is not only to report metrics but also to generate business insights, recommendations, and decision-support intelligence that help improve customer retention, increase revenue, and optimize marketing campaigns.

The project combines:

- SQL Analytics
- Python Data Analysis
- Power BI Dashboards
- Business Intelligence Frameworks
- AI-Assisted Recommendations

---

# Business Problem

Banks often face challenges such as:

- High customer churn
- Inefficient marketing campaigns
- Difficulty identifying high-value customers
- Lack of data-driven decision making

This project addresses these challenges by answering:

### Business Question 1

Why are customers churning and which customer segments are at highest risk?

### Business Question 2

Which customers generate the highest business value?

### Business Question 3

How effective are marketing campaigns in generating customer engagement and conversions?

# Analytical Frameworks Used

# Key Findings

## Customer Churn

- Overall churn rate: 52%
- British Columbia has the highest churn rate (56.86%)

### Recommendation

Launch province-specific retention programs and loyalty incentives.

---

## Revenue Concentration

Top customers contribute a disproportionate share of revenue.

### Recommendation

Create premium customer retention and rewards programs.

---

## Campaign Performance

- CTR: 50.36%
- Conversion Rate: 49.87%

### Recommendation

Scale high-performing campaigns and optimize customer targeting.
